Hi, I'm Logan McNatt, creator of A Joyful Life! I grew up in a family of six as the youngest of four boys.

One of my greatest passions is ballroom dancing! I've been doing it for 2.5 years now. I am currently on Ensemble 1, one of the 5 performance teams at BYU. Ballroom dancing is unique in that it has both a performance form and a competitive form, known as DanceSport. I have competed in the styles of Standard and Latin, with a desire to be a National Amatuer 10-Dance Champion!

I am an extrovert and love making new friends! I feel like every stranger is just a friend I havent been able to meet yet.
